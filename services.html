Rubber Safety Matting

Our rubber saftey matting is designed to reduce the risk of injury from slip, trips and falls. Manufactured from a combination of recycled and virgin rubber and comes in different colours. The matting is in a tile format which allows replacement of a damaged tile.

Installation

Theres no need to worry about the installation we can handle all that for you, from sourcing the materials and equipment to removing excess soil and debris we have it all under control.

Foundation

With a strong concrete foundation your playhouse will not be easily damaged in a storm or otherwise and will last. Combined with rubber matting this makes for a great safe play area for the kids and is also very low maintance with no trying to cut grass around the playhouse and no pulling weeds from the matting and it has a concrete base below.

Accessibility

We have a wide range of colours and sizes to suit any playhouse size. We can also provide you with a custom design to suit your needs.
